Our Story

Passionate About Nutrition Since 2015

DIET LAB by Priya is a specialist dietetic consultancy offering evidence-based, client-focused nutritional care. Led by Priya, a UK-verified dietitian with expertise in gastrointestinal health and general dietetic practice, the service is committed to delivering high-quality, outcome-driven support for individuals and healthcare organisations. As the founder of Diet Lab by Priya, a YouTube channel dedicated to dietetic education, Priya’s mission is to promote healthy lifestyles and empower individuals with the knowledge to make informed nutritional decisions. Her approach has shown proven success in delivering interventions that result in measurable clinical improvements, particularly in obesity and diabetes management. The practice operates in alignment with NHS standards and supports NHS trusts with specialist dietetic input. We coordinate with GPs when needed to ensure integrated care. DIET LAB is fully GDPR compliant and holds Data Security and Protection Toolkit (DSPT) status. We work with selected UK health insurance providers to enhance service accessibility. Appointments can be booked through our website or via WhatsApp. At DIET LAB by Priya, clients receive professional, personalised dietary support built on clinical expertise and patient empowerment.

About Priya

Priya is a UK-verified specialist dietitian registered with the Health and Care Professions Council (HCPC), and a member of the British Dietetic Association (BDA) and the Indian Dietetic Association (IDA). She began her professional journey in 2015 after completing her Master’s in Clinical Nutrition and Dietetics, and has since developed extensive experience across diverse clinical settings. Her career spans outpatient and inpatient care, with roles in diabetes specialities, gastroenterology, paediatrics, endocrinology, and acute hospital care. This broad foundation has shaped her holistic, evidence-based approach to nutritional therapy. Priya has a particular clinical interest in gastroenterology, offering specialist support for digestive health and related conditions. She has consistently delivered impactful dietary interventions that contribute to improved outcomes in obesity, diabetes, and gastrointestinal disorders. Her practice is rooted in patient-centred care, clear communication, and long-term lifestyle management. Priya is also the founder of Diet Lab by Priya, a YouTube platform dedicated to dietetic education, where she promotes public understanding of nutrition and health. In the UK, she collaborates with NHS trusts, supports communication with GPs for integrated care, and works with health insurance providers to make dietetic services more accessible. Her consultancy is GDPR compliant and holds Data Security and Protection Toolkit (DSPT) status with Standards Met. With a strong academic foundation and over a decade of hands-on clinical experience, Priya provides expert, personalised care designed to empower individuals and improve lives through nutrition.
